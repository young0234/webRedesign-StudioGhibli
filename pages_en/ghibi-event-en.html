Suzuki and Ghibli Exhibition

#NiceToSeeYouAgain #ReservationRequired
Apr 25, 2025 (Fri) - Jun 29, 2025 (Sun)
Nagano Prefectural Art Museum

"Toshio Suzuki Through Books" With Takahata Isao and Hayao Miyazaki, producer Toshio Suzuki brought many globally beloved Ghibli films to life. This exhibition focuses on the books Suzuki has read and the eras he lived through, from postwar Nagoya to the Showa, Heisei, and Reiwa eras. Studio Ghibli's 3D Exhibits

May 27, 2025 (Tue) - September 23, 2025 (Tue)
Warehouse TERRADA B&C HALL

Studio Ghibli films, including *The Boy and the Heron*, have been released in over 80 countries and regions around the world. Behind these films are passionate individuals who devoted themselves to sharing Ghibli’s magic. This exhibit traces how overseas partners brought Ghibli works to life, featuring famous film scenes recreated as 3D sculptures. Toshio Suzuki and Ghibli Exhibition

April 25, 2025 (Fri) - June 29, 2025 (Sun)
Nagano Art Museum

*Toshio Suzuki Through Books* Toshio Suzuki, the producer who worked alongside Isao Takahata and Hayao Miyazaki to bring Ghibli’s legendary films to life, is in the spotlight. This exhibition focuses on the books Suzuki read growing up in postwar Nagoya through the Showa, Heisei, and Reiwa eras. Through history books, nonfiction, and critiques he has read with sincere curiosity, we’ll explore how these shaped his thinking and creative process. Learn how he became a key figure in Ghibli as a producer and editor.

Friday Road Show and Ghibli

April 27, 2025 (Thu) - July 6, 2025 (Sun)
Nagasaki Art Museum

This exhibit looks back on the history of the Japanese TV program *Friday Road Show*, while celebrating the charm of Studio Ghibli films. Since its debut in 1985, the show aired *Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind* in 1986 and has since broadcast over 200 Ghibli titles. Discover how this program helped Ghibli build its reputation and legacy through nostalgic memories and archives from the Showa, Heisei, and Reiwa eras.
