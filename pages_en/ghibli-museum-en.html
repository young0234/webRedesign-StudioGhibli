About the Ghibli Museum

This is a place not only for fans of Studio Ghibli,
but also for those who want more than just a display of past works like a "museum of the past."
It’s a space where you can enjoy just by looking, and understand the spirit of the artists.

Ticket Types / Sales Period

★ Ticket Types

  • Named reservation tickets with specified entry date/time (Entry Exchange Ticket = Voucher)
  • Only reservation exchange tickets issued by JTBGMT (JTB Global Marketing & Travel) are valid, and the ticket must include the entry date, the name of the entrant, and the passport number.
  • Entry times: 10:00, 12:00, 14:00, 16:00 (4 times per day)

★ Sales Period

  • Tickets are sold for 4 months starting from the current sales month.
  • Example: From July 1 → Tickets for July, August, September, and October can be reserved.
How to Purchase / Quantity

★ How to Purchase

  • Cash sales only at designated travel agencies.

★ Purchase Limit

  • Up to 6 tickets per person.
Purchase Procedure
  • At purchase: Present ID, and provide desired entry date and names of all entrants.
  • At entry: Exchange the voucher for an entry ticket at the Ghibli Museum entrance to enter.
  • For details regarding purchase of entry exchange tickets, please contact the designated travel agencies.

How to Get Here

1-1-83 Shimorenjaku, Mitaka City, Tokyo 181-0013, Japan

Public Transportation

Train
Take the JR Chuo Line to JR Mitaka Station, about 20 minutes from JR Shinjuku Station.
The museum is a 15-minute walk from the South Exit of JR Mitaka Station.
Community Bus (Mitaka City Bus)
A shuttle bus runs every 15 minutes during opening hours between JR Mitaka Station and the museum.
- Circulates between Mitaka Station and Ghibli Museum.
- Returns via Meisei Academy after passing the museum.

By Car

Car
There is no dedicated parking lot at the museum site.
Please avoid coming by car.
Visitors with disabilities who need to come by car should contact us in advance.
