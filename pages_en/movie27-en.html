  • This scar, I made it myself.

    Mahito Maki

    An 11-year-old boy in 6th grade of elementary school who lost his mother in a hospital fire.
    He experiences mysterious events with his father, who remarried his aunt.

  • Do you remember the things from the other world?
    Forget it, forget it. Usually, everyone forgets.
    The Heron

    A heron living at Mahito Maki’s maternal family mansion.

  • My precious son was hurt,
    how could I stand by quietly!
    Shoichi Maki

    Father of Mahito Maki.
    After his wife Hisako passed away, he remarried her younger sister, Natsuko.
    A wealthy man who runs a large-scale military manufacturing factory.

  • Natsuko

    Hisako’s younger sister.
    Mahito’s aunt and new stepmother.

  • Hisako

    Mahito Maki’s biological mother and Natsuko’s older sister.
    She died in a hospital fire.

  • Of course I’ll come back. Giving birth to you is an amazing thing!
    Himi

    A girl Mahito Maki meets in another world.
    She can control fire.

  • How you stack the stones is up to you.
    Great-grandfather

    The great-grandfather of Hisako and Natsuko’s mother.
    He was so obsessed with reading books that rumors said he lost his mind over it,
    and later he was reported missing.
