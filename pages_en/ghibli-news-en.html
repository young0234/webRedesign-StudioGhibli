"Editorial Ghibli News" May 2025 Issue

This month, as in previous issues, we greet you with "Editorial Ghibli News." Thank you for your continued support. This year, 2025, marks the 90th birthday of Director Isao Takahata. At the same time, 2025 is also the 80th anniversary of the end of World War II in the Pacific, which greatly influenced Takahata’s life, philosophy, and works.
