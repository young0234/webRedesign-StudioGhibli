Q
What is the origin of the name "Ghibli"?
A

The spelling "GHIBLI" comes from an Italian word meaning a hot desert wind blowing in the Sahara.
Also, the correct pronunciation in Italian is closer to "Ghibli" being pronounced as "Ghibli" (with a soft "g").

Q
Is it possible to tour the studio?
A

We are sorry, but we do not accept any requests for studio tours.
Since there is no official tour course at Studio Ghibli, allowing tours would distract our staff from their work.
Also, some works that have not been released yet would be restricted from public viewing.

Q
I would like to request a lecture from Hayao Miyazaki.
A

We apologize, but Mr. Miyazaki does not give lectures.
Although he did give lectures about 10 years ago, due to many requests and to focus on his main work, he has stopped lecture activities.
We appreciate your understanding.

Q
I want to turn my work into an animation.
A

Original works are not accepted.
Studio Ghibli receives various creations (scripts, proposals, drawings, videos, music, etc.) from people.
However, unless requested in advance, we do not review these submissions and must dispose of them.
This is to avoid misunderstandings regarding creativity and rights between the studio's works and submitted materials.
This measure protects both parties and is unavoidable.

Q
Where are the settings for the films?
A

Officially, there are few Studio Ghibli films that are set in real places.
Most works include various locations partially, and many scenes are entirely fictional.
Therefore, you may not find identical scenery when visiting these places.

Q
Where do Team NACS members appear in "Howl's Moving Castle"?
A

Team NACS members voice some characters in the film.
Examples include A.B. soldiers who go to pick up Sophie, characters interacting with Lettie, and other supporting roles.
Various scenes include their voice performances throughout the movie.

Q
Where can I buy Ghibli merchandise?
A

In 2021, the Studio Ghibli Museum online shop opened.
They sell original and exclusive Ghibli merchandise (some with limited quantities).
Books, DVDs, and CDs are also available.
Ghibli products can be found in toy stores, souvenir shops, malls, specialty stores, and tourist spots, with the "Donguri Republic" being the main store.
Visit http://www.benelic.com/donguri/ for a store list.
The online shop "Soranoe Store" offers online sales and limited items.
CDs and DVDs can also be purchased at music shops and online stores.
Ghibli books are available at bookstores, with many stocked at the Ghibli book specialty shops.

Q
Do you sell animation cels?
A

Animation cels previously handled by the Ghibli Museum are no longer available.
Currently, there are no official sales of cels.

Q
Are there music scores for Ghibli films?
A

Original soundtrack scores are not available.
However, arrangements for piano solo, brass band, etc. can be found from various music publishers.
Please check with music or record stores.

Q
What is "On Your Mark"?
A

"On Your Mark" is a short film first shown in 1995 during a screening of "Whisper of the Heart" (directed by Yoshifumi Kondo).
It is a promotional animation for the song of the same name by CHAGE & ASKA,
written and directed by Hayao Miyazaki, lasting 6 minutes and 40 seconds.
It was also shown during the 1995 CHAGE & ASKA concert tour.

Q
Does the book "Fairy Tale" really exist?
A

The "Fairy Tale" book that Shizuku borrowed from the library in "Whisper of the Heart" was fictional, based on the original work by Aoi Hiiragi.
Although fairy-related tales like elves and dwarfs exist overseas,
the specific story is entirely an original work by director Miyazaki.
