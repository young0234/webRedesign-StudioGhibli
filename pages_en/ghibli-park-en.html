About Ghibli Park

Ghibli Park is a theme park representing the world of Studio Ghibli, located across the vast grounds of the 2005 Aichi Expo Commemorative Park (Moricoro Park).
Take a walk through the park and discover various attractions.

Entering Ghibli Park

The following items are required for entry:

★ E-Ticket

  • To display your ticket, click the URL in the email titled “Ticket Display Start Notification” received after reservation. Please bookmark the URL.

★ Reservation Holder (Applicant)

  • A smartphone with internet access is required to display the passport ticket. (Some entrances at Ghibli Park may not have free Wi-Fi.)
  • If you don’t have a smartphone with internet access, be sure to bring the My Page (which shows QR codes and details for all group members).
  • PDF files and screenshots are not accepted.
  • My Page (QR code and reservation details) is accessible from the URL in the confirmation email. You will need the password and phone number used during purchase to log in.
  • All group members must enter each area/facility of Ghibli Park together.
Important Information About E-Tickets
  • Ghibli Park e-tickets are displayed using MOALA Ticket provided by Noriter Inc. (No app installation required)
  • An internet-connected smartphone is required to display the ticket. (Recommended: iOS 14.0+ with Safari, Android 6.0+ with Chrome)
  • Data charges incurred from ticket display and entry are the customer's responsibility.
  • All group members must enter each area together.
  • Please ensure your phone is fully charged when visiting.
  • The QR code on the e-ticket is specially processed. Printed copies or screenshots cannot be used for entry.
  • Click here for instructions on using MOALA Ticket.
Important Notes
  • Admission tickets are only valid on the specified date and time.
  • After reservation, changes or refunds for the date, time, ticket type, or quantity are not allowed. Please check details carefully before booking.
  • Purchasing tickets for resale purposes is strictly prohibited and may violate laws. Entry will be denied if tickets are found to be resold, including via auctions.
  • Lost, damaged, or stolen tickets will not be reissued for any reason.
  • Some areas may be inaccessible due to congestion or other issues.
  • Please cooperate with our COVID-19 prevention measures. Be sure to check the official website for “COVID-19 Safety Guidelines” before purchasing.
Notices
  • Re-entry is not allowed for each area except for “Valley of Witches.” (From April tickets onward, the O-Sanpo Day Pass Premium allows re-entry to all areas except Ghibli’s Grand Warehouse.)
  • Some activities and attractions require additional fees.
  • Some facilities may be unavailable during rainy weather.
  • Certain facilities and activities are restricted to children.
  • Photography and video recording inside the park is prohibited except in designated areas. Please refrain from using flash even where photography is permitted indoors.
  • The following are prohibited from entering Ghibli Park:
    • Any behavior that disturbs other guests and/or interferes with park operations — including unauthorized tour guiding or other commercial activities
    • Unauthorized promotional activities (including photo and video shooting for publicity purposes)
  • Please refer to the official website for more details.
